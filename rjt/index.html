About Jamestown

Our campaign celebrates Jamestown as a vibrant community where heritage and creativity thrive.

Jamestown, one of Accra's most historic districts, is a captivating blend of tradition and modernity. This coastal community tells the story of Ghana's rich history through its colonial architecture, bustling fishing harbor, and vibrant street life.

We highlight its bustling fishing harbour, the iconic lighthouse, and the beloved Kenkey House as symbols of tradition and pride. These landmarks represent the enduring spirit of a community deeply connected to its maritime roots and culinary heritage.

At the heart of it all are the people who embody Jamestown's spirit—fishermen, artists, traders, and families who have called this place home for generations.

Jamestown Highlights

Discover the iconic landmarks that define Jamestown's unique character

Bustling Fishing Harbour

Fishing Harbour

The heart of Jamestown's economy and culture, where colorful fishing boats return daily with fresh catch, and the lively fish market buzzes with activity from dawn till dusk.

Iconic Jamestown Lighthouse

Jamestown Lighthouse

This historic lighthouse, built in the 1930s, offers panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and the surrounding community—a symbol of guidance and resilience for generations.

Beloved Kenkey House

Kenkey House

A culinary institution serving this traditional Ghanaian staple, Kenkey House represents the community's food heritage and serves as a gathering place for locals and visitors alike.

Cultural Celebrations

Experience the vibrant festivals that bring Jamestown's streets to life

Homowo Festival

A festival of resilience celebrated by the Ga people, Homowo ("hooting at hunger") commemorates a historic harvest after a period of famine. The streets come alive with dancing, drumming, and the traditional sprinkling of "kpokpoi" (corn dish).

Chale Wote Festival

This dynamic street art festival transforms Jamestown into a global stage of art and music. For one week each year, the district becomes an open-air gallery with installations, performances, and creative expressions from local and international artists.

Community Spirit

Beyond formal festivals, Jamestown's creative energy flows daily through boxing gyms, photography studios, music sessions, and impromptu gatherings that showcase the community's enduring spirit and innovative resilience.
